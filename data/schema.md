# Data Schema

## Daily_Mess_Data
- Date
- Breakfast_Count
- Lunch_Count
- Dinner_Count
- Total_Students

## Prediction_Output
- Predicted_Total
- Prediction_Date

## Queue_Status
- Current_Count
- Queue_Level (Low / Medium / High)
- Last_Updated

## Assumptions
- Lunch is the peak meal
- Short-term history reflects demand patterns
- Queue levels are based on predicted totals
